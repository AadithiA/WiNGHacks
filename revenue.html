Premium Subscription

In the premium subscription program, the user is prompted to enter their financial details, such as their income, and how much they would like to save. The program will then calculate what financial habits the user should adopt, and help the user track these habits.

Advertisements

The finance habit tracker will include advertisements from our sponsors, that are not distracting to the user, but will still help our product generate revenue.

Partnernships

In the future, we would like to partner with banking services, in order to enable users to link their financial information through their bank accounts. This way, users will be able to see their financial information in one place and receive updates on their finance goals as they view their banking information.
