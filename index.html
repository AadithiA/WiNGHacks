What is CapySpace?

CapySpace is a safe space to relax and learn more about the importance of mental health and wellness within the ambience of a capybara hot springs.To practice wellness, you can record journal entries as emotional catharsis, to organize chaotic thoughts, or just for fun. :)

Why It Matters

As the topic of mental health becomes more common and society becomes more accepting of these issues, it is important more than ever that we are educated and aware about these disorders, knowing about the proper resources and how to access them, and most importantly, knowing reliable strategies and tips on how to manage them.
